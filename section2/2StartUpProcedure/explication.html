From now on, you will program the robot in python.

Before putting your hands on the code, let's see the functionnalities of the development environment that you will use.

The environment consists of five windows, that you can open and close at your will with the buttons on left toolbar.

  • The "Simulation" shows the view of Webots. It can be open/close with the button representing a 3D cube.
  • The "Information" window regroup all you need to know about the exercise: the theory and the different tasks you will be requested to complete. Its button is the "I"
  • The "IDE" is where you will write your code. Its button is the "< >"
  • The "Terminal", will display the outputs and errors of the simulation. It is represented by this sign: ">_". If you want to clean the terminal, click on it and press "CTRL + L".
  • Finally, the "Robot window" will show the value of the motors and sensors of the robot. It opens via the dial-shaped button

Please note that the IDE, terminal and robot window will only be active once the simulation is loaded as depend on it.

Also note that if you close or reload the page, all the modifications you made to the code will be lost.

Do not hesitate to play with the layout by closing, moving, resizing windows, such that you feel good to work with it!

Finally, we strongly advise you to put the whole development environment in full screen. To do so, click on the "Full screen" button on the bottom left corner of the environment.
