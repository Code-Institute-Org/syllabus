In this exercise, you will program a NAO robot with Blockly.

Your goal is to get the NAO to the finish line.

To do that, you have to make a chain of blocks

Just drag and drop blocks in the white area and snap them together.

When you want to test a chain of blocks, press the "Run" button.

If you want to reset the position of the robot. press the "Reset" button

Finally, if you want to remove some blocks, simply drag and drop them back to the area on the left of the screen.
